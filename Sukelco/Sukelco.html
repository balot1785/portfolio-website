Wireless Bridging System

Apr. 2019-May 2019

This project aims to enhance operational efficiency and connectivity for SUKELCO by establishing a reliable wireless LAN infrastructure in remote offices. This will enable seamless communication and data sharing across different remote offices.

Project Deliverables

  • 6 Wireless Broadband Radios: To provide wireless connectivity between the head office and the sub offices.
  • 4 PoE switch: Connected throughout the network.

Project Scope

  • Wireless network infrastructure: Establishment of a wireless network link to support the surveillance cameras and enable real-time data transmission.
  • Testing and commissioning: Conduct comprehensive testing and commissioning procedures to ensure the system's functionality and performance.
