City Wide Wireless Video Surveillance System

Dec. 2023-Feb. 2024

This project aims to enhance the security of Digos City by implementing a comprehensive wireless video surveillance system. This network of cameras will provide real-time monitoring of critical locations, enabling proactive response to potential threats and incidents.

Project Deliverables

  • 5 Fixed Bullet Cameras: For wide-area surveillance with static views.
  • 7 PTZ (Pan-Tilt-Zoom) Cameras: For dynamic monitoring with the ability to pan, tilt, and zoom.
  • 10 Wireless Broadband Radios: For data transmission across the surveillance network.
  • 1 Command Center: A centralized location for monitoring and managing camera feeds.

Project Scope

  • Design and installation of a wireless video surveillance network covering key areas in Digos City.
  • Integration with existing city infrastructure for seamless operation.
  • Training for city personnel on system operation and maintenance.
  • Ongoing support and maintenance for the first year of operation.
